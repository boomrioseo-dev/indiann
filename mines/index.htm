What Is the Mines Game?

This is a simple but quite exciting crash game. The screen is a 5×5 grid consisting of only 25 tiles. Each tile can contain either a star or a mine, and what is behind it is not predictable in advance. Accordingly, you need to find as many stars as possible without running into a mine.
At first, you set the number of mines on the board, this affects the degree of risk and winnings. The more mines you program, the higher the reward, but the risk will also increase. You can stop the game at any point in it.

Parimatch Mines Rules

The steps to play are as follows:

  • Before starting to play, you must first select the number of mines on the field (1 to 24);
  • After that, a field opens with tiles (the entire field is 5×5, that is, 25 cells), which are either safe or have mines hidden in them;
  • You open tiles one by one. If you land on a safe one, the coefficient increases;
  • You can stop at any time and collect the winnings, or continue opening tiles further in the hope of increasing it;
  • If you land on a mine under a tile, you lose the entire bet, and the game starts over.
