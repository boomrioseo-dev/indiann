Betting Odds

We provide competitive odds on all the major sports and betting markets. Within the platform, you will see the decimal odds format, one of the world’s most popular and easiest forms of betting odds.

In decimal odds, the odds represent the total return for every 1 INR wagered, including your original stake. To take an example, if you bet 100 INR at odds of 2.50, you would receive a total return of 250 INR, which consists of your original 100 INR stake plus a profit of 150 INR.
