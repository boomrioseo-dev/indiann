Types of eSports Bets at Parimatch

Our sportsbook provides multiple betting options, offering you the ability to be flexible. Below, you can see available options:

  • Match Winner. Bet on which team wins the match. Present in CS2, Dota 2, League of Legends, Valorant, and Rainbow Six;
  • Map Winner. Bet on which team wins the map in the match series. Present in CS2, Valorant, and Rainbow Six;
  • First Kill (First Blood). Bet on which team/player will get the first kill in the match. Present in CS2, Dota 2, and Valorant;
  • Total Kills (Team/Player). Bet on whether this player will go over or under a certain number of kills. Present in CS2 and Valorant;
  • Total Team Kills. Bet on the total number of kills a team is going to score. Present in CS2 and Dota 2;
  • Over/Under Rounds or Maps. Bet on whether rounds (CS2 and Rainbow Six) or maps (Dota 2) will be over or under a number (typically set by the bookmaker). Common in CS2 and Rainbow Six;
  • Correct Score. Simply predict the correct score of a match series (e.g., 2-0, 2-1). Common in all major games.
