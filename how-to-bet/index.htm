How to Play at Parimatch?

The platform offers not only betting, but also slots. To find out how to play Parimatch slots, follow the instructions:

  1. Click the “Sign up” button and create your personal account.

How to Register Account?

To fully use the platform, you need to register. To do this, read the instructions below:

  1. Hit the yellow “Parimatch Sign up” button to initiate the registration process.
  2. Enter the required information, namely your work phone number, and create a password.
  3. Confirm that you are 18 years old.
  4. Click “Sign up”.

How to Verify Account?

Account verification allows you to further secure it, and also gives you the opportunity to withdraw funds. To verify your profile:

  1. Click on the “Log in” button and login to your account.
  2. Press the “Personal Account” button to open your profile settings.
  3. Fill all the required information. Save changes.
  4. Click on the “Account Verification” button.
  5. Upload photos of your valid documents. You can upload a photo of your ID, passport, driver’s license, or utility bill.

If the verification on Parimatch not working or take too much time, contact Parimatch via customer support.

How to Deposit at Parimatch?

Making a deposit on the platform is a fairly easy process. Read the guide below if you don’t know how to do this:

  1. Use “Log in” button to log in to your personal account.
  2. Press the green “Deposit” button in the top right corner.
  3. Choose preferred payment method and write amount you want to deposit. The Parimatch minimum deposit is only 300 INR.
  4. Click the “Deposit” button and follow on the screen instructions.

How to Withdraw Money?

If you have won and verified your account, here is how you can withdraw your winnings:

  1. Open your personal profile. To do it, click on the “Personal Account” button in the right menu.
  2. Press “Withdraw”.
  3. Choose your preferred withdrawal method and write the amount you want to withdraw.
  4. Confirm the transaction.
